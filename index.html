Why It Matters

Why offline-first matters.

  • 💥Cloud tools get breached. Your data was never yours to begin with.
  • 💳Subscriptions expire. Features you rely on disappear behind a paywall.
  • 🧾Accounts mean liability. Every sign-up is data you've handed over permanently.
  • 📡Telemetry is always on. Even "privacy-focused" apps track usage patterns.
  • 🔗Lock-in is the business model. Exporting your own data is never easy.

Flowtive exists because the tools people use most — password managers, generators, converters, calculators — don't need the internet. They never did. Giving them network access doesn't make them better. It makes them a liability.

Every tool in this suite is a deliberate answer to that problem. Open source so you can verify the code. Single-file so you can inspect everything. Offline so your data has nowhere to go but stay.
