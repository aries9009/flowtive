03 — Workflow

From chaos to compiled prompt in three moves.

No onboarding. No tutorial. Open the app and your first prompt is ten seconds away.

01

Save it.

⌘N opens the editor. Paste or write your prompt, pick a category, add tags. ⌘+Enter saves. Done in ten seconds.

02

Make it reusable.

Replace specifics with {{variables}}. Quick-insert chips handle the common ones — {{tone}}, {{topic}}, {{audience}}. Every variable auto-detected as you type.

03

Test and copy.

Open the test panel, fill your variables, watch the output compile live. One click copies to clipboard — ready for Claude, ChatGPT, or whatever you're running.
