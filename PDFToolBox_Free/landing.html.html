How it Works

Three steps.
Zero uploads.

PDFToolbox processes everything locally using WebAssembly-powered PDF engines baked directly into the page. There is no step where your file leaves your device.

01 — Drop

Drop your PDF

Drag a PDF onto the dropzone, or click to browse. The file is read locally by your browser. No upload happens. File info appears instantly.

02 — Configure

Pick your operation

Switch between Split, Extract, or Merge. Set page ranges, click thumbnails to select, or queue files for merging. Everything is visual and immediate.

03 — Download

Download the result

Hit the action button. Your browser processes the PDF in milliseconds and hands the file directly to your downloads folder — no server ever involved.

Privacy isn't a feature — it's the architecture.

Most "free" PDF tools upload your documents to servers you don't control. You hand over a sensitive contract or a medical record and hope for the best. PDFToolbox is structurally different: your files are read into browser memory, processed by JavaScript running on your machine, and downloaded directly to your device. There are no servers, no logs, no analytics on your files, and no way for us to see your documents even if we wanted to.
